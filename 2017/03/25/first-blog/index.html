Live My Life And Experience Freedom

Since my first time to build my blog in Beijing, it has been 2 years, how time flies! Howerver, instead deploy wordexpress in Aliyun, this time I decide to use hexo which is a static blog framework to express myself, and to conquer my lazy property, I will spare my efforts to live my remaining life with words. After many years, maybe it will be exciting to review my life through the words in this world of freedom.
