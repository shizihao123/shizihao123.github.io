How to use Hexo to build your blog on Github

This document will teach you how to build your blog on github based on the hexo framework(which is a static blog framework).

Quick Start

Step 1

Create a new repository in github, and the repository name must be your github usename with the suffix of “.github.io”.
For example, my username is shizihao123, the new repository is shizihao123.github.io. Open https://username.github.io and you will see your blog!!!

Step 2

Install Hexo

Step 3

Set up and Configure Hexo
Key point : Open _config.yml, set your url to https://username.github.io and set root as /.

Step 4

Edit your blog according to the hexo guide.

Step 5

Use the basic command to render, generate and deploy your blog.

Cong! You have finished the building of your blog with hexo, and write what you want now !

Interview

It’s very lucky to get graduate exemption into NJU, and it’s really tight to achieve enough knowledge and ability in the short 3 years. Besides burying myself into papers, learning the info of job applying is very important. After all, I must get a job to feed my girl friend and this is my first priority after getting my master degree. But doing some deep research and keep learning new skills will never stop in my life. If I can get the goal of financial freedom some day, maybe I will consider to proceed for PhD and that’s a later story.

Microsoft

When I was a junior in NUPT, I had a chance to attend a on-site summer intern interview in Microsoft Suzhou, which is an academy of engineering for projects like Bing of MS. Now when I turn back to the experience, I feel it’s so lucky for me to pass the online programming tests and fight into the final intern interview at that time but now I just face my continuous failure in online programming tests during NJU’s graduate student’s life. Algorithm is really the key point both in online programming tests and the on-site interview of “Flag”. The understanding of the problem description, the building of model, the programming skills of each detail and the continuous optimization of time complexity should be training more in the regular practice. Programming exercises and skills review will be one of my regular jobs in my graduate student’s life. Luckily, Min has the same goal with me, and that will make me have tons of motivation.

BAT

Also, due to my courage and attempts during the undergraduate period, I have gone through all the online interviews of BAT (Baidu, Alibaba, Tencent) which are Domestic Internet business giants. But maybe it was too young for me to become a engineer in the company at that time, I didn’t even know the concept of “code refactor” and all the interview failed in the reexamine period . However, after more practice in intern life, I have improved a lot, and I will record my job interview in my later story.
