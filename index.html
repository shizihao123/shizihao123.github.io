Live My Life And Experience Freedom

Since my first time to build my blog in Beijing, it has been 2 years, how time flies! Howerver, instead deploy wordexpress in Aliyun, this time I decide to use hexo which is a static blog framework to express myself, and to conquer my lazy property, I will spare my efforts to live my remaining life with words. So the question is why use English instead of Chinese, one of the reason is it’s enough to speak Chinese in life every day and mybe it’s freshness when use English and another is I just don’t wanna lose the english skills .There are many things to pick up from my step into the NJUPT and I will supplement my past life or work in the following. After many years, maybe it will be exciting to review my life through the words in this world of freedom.

interview

It’s very lucky to get graduate exemption into NJU, and it’sreally precious to achieve enough knowledge and ability in the short 3 years. Besides burrying into the books, learning the info of job applying is very important.After all, I must get a job to raise up my girl friend and this is my first priority after getting my master degree. If I get financial freedom one day, I will consider to do research in the remainder of my life and that’s a later story.

Microsoft

When I was a junior in NJUPT, I had a chance to go for a on-site summer intern interview in Microsoft Suzhou, which is an enginnering center for projects like Bing of MS. Now I turn back to the experince, I feel it’s so lucky for me to pass the online programming tests and fight into the final intern interview at old time but now I just face my continuous failure in NJU’s graduate student’s life. The algorithm is really the key point both in online programming tests and the on-site interview of “Flag”. The understanding of the problem desccription, the building of model, the programming skills of each detail and the continuous optimization of time complexity should be trainning more in the regular excercising. SO excersing and review will be one of my regular items in my graduate student’s life. Luckily, My girl friend have the same goal with me), and that will be tons of motivation.

BAT

Also, due to my courage in the bachelor time, I have all online interview inBAT (Baidu, Alibaba, Tencent) which are Domestic Internet business giants. But maybe it was too young for me to become a engineer in the company at that time, I didn’t know the concept of “code refactor”.But now, I become more expericed, so I will record my job hunter experince in my later story.
